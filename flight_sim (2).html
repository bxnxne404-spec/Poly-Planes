Take off from the home runway, keep your speed above stall or you'll sink, and explore the three airfields scattered across the island. Taxi up to a terminal gate and press B (or use the on-screen button) to swap into the passenger jet. Watch out — there's roughly a 1-in-7 chance any takeoff triggers an in-flight malfunction: you'll be thrown into the cockpit view and have to solve a quick math problem by dialing a gauge to the right number with the arrow keys before time runs out, or you'll crash. Hitting the ground (or failing that repair) will reset you at the home airport in a ball of fire!
